Soup

A warm, comforting chicken soup made with tender chicken, vegetables, and herbs.

Perfect for cold days or when you need a soothing meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 whole chicken breast (or 2 thighs), boneless
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 bay leaf

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, carrots, and celery, and sauté for 5–7 minutes until soft.
  3. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Place the chicken into the pot, then pour in the chicken broth.
  5. Add bay leaf, thyme, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat to a simmer, cover, and cook for 25–30 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked.
  7. Remove the chicken, shred it with a fork, and return it to the pot.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.
